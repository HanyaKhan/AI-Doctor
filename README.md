# AI-Doctor
Doctor AI is a medical chatbot, which is powered by AI and analyses symptoms, asks follow-up questions akin to a doctor, forecasts potential diseases, and presents a  health report in PDF format.
Doctor AI is an intelligent AI-powered medical assistant designed to simulate a real doctor–patient consultation. The system enables patients to describe their symptoms through a conversational chatbot interface. Based on the provided information, the AI asks relevant follow-up questions—similar to the process a doctor follows during a clinical diagnosis—to gather more accurate details about the patient’s condition.

After analyzing the collected symptoms and responses, the system predicts possible diseases and provides basic health recommendations or potential treatment suggestions. To enhance usability and documentation, the platform automatically generates a detailed medical report in PDF format, summarizing the symptoms, AI analysis, predicted conditions, and suggested guidance.

The primary objective of this project is to demonstrate how artificial intelligence and conversational systems can assist in delivering preliminary medical insights, improving accessibility to healthcare information, and helping users better understand potential health issues before seeking professional medical consultation. By combining AI-driven dialogue with symptom analysis, the system aims to create a simple and user-friendly digital healthcare support tool.
